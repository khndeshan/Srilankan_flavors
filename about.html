About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Sri Lankan Flavors started as a small family-run eatery in Colombo. Today, we are proud to serve thousands of happy customers every month while maintaining the same traditional recipes passed down for generations.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To become the leading Sri Lankan cuisine restaurant recognized worldwide for authentic taste and hospitality.

Mission: To provide our customers with fresh, delicious, and authentic Sri Lankan meals, delivered with warmth and excellent service.
