About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Sri Lankan Flavors started as a small family-run eatery in Colombo. Today, we serve thousands of happy customers while keeping traditional recipes alive.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To become a leading Sri Lankan cuisine restaurant recognized worldwide for authentic taste and hospitality.

Mission: To provide our customers with fresh, delicious, authentic meals with warmth and excellent service.
